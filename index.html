PROJECT #1


For this first project we were tasked with writing a "flarf" poem around a Google search of a text we had sent recently. The text I sent was "pano picture of the basement or murphy will fart" in reference to my aunt renovating her basement and me asking for a picture or else murphy (her dog) would fart. It was very silly and prompted a Google search about dogs being scared of basements. I took that and ran with it, creating a spooky basement poem.

PERSONAL WORK


A lot of the work I have done over the years centers around the difficulty of finding music posters that matched my aesthetic. At the last college I attened one of the projects for one of my classes entailed us making promotional material for an upcoming show of an artist we enjoyed. After completing that assignment I though to myself, why don't I start designing my own posters for myself and friends and that is where this poster comes into play. I made it for my ex-boyfriend before I moved back to Chicago from New Orleans. "Isn't She Lovely" by Stevie Wonder is his favorite song. He wanted to hang the poster up in his room, which featured a lot of navy and yellow, hence the use of both in this poster. Tuesday, November 1st was our first date and Urban Roots was the location. The stars were my "artist mark" one could say as I am obsessed with the shape.
